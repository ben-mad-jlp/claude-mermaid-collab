- **The Orchestration group exists.** `src/services/leaf-executor.ts:711-717` defines group `key:'orchestration'`, `label:'Orchestration'`, `defaultCollapsed:false`, `kinds: ORCHESTRATION_NODE_KINDS` (`['forge','conductor','planner']`).
